- Dependency update: DocumentFormat.OpenXml 2.18.0 -> 2.20.0

## 2.2
### Added
- Show or hide, print or not, Gridlines and headings

- Bumped dependencies version to current latest since the reason to lower it no longer applies.

## 2.1
